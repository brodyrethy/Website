Term Definitions

Arboreal

Tree dweller.

Brachiation

Movement by a swinging of the arms from one hold to the next.

Cognitive Dissonance

The state of discomfort when two or more modes of thought contradict each other. The clashing cognitions may include ideas, beliefs, or the knowledge that one has behaved in a certain way..

Dimorphic

Two distinct physical forms (aka the classic categorization of male/female).

Extant

Means ``existing" (antonym: Extinct).

Hominids

An individual member of the taxonomic family of primates known as a \textbf{great ape}.

Hominoid

An individual member of the superfamily Hominoidea known as an \textbf{ape}.

Human

An individual member of the genus Homo.

Ironclad

Secure, firm, or unbrakeable..

Tribe

A (taxonomic) rank above genus, but below family and sub-family..

Prehensile

An organ that has adapted to grasping or holding.

Pygmy

Something of short stature.

Quadrumanous

Having opposable first digits on both the feet and the hands, used for gripping and climbing.

Semibrachiation

Brachiation assisted by use of a prehensile limb.
