Albums I've Heard

I give out a lot of 10/10s because I listen to music 24/7 while in public. This means that many albums end up being associated with a certain time and place. I end up being sentimental about certain albums, hence why they get a 10/10 even though some aren't exactly "perfect". My idea of a 10/10 is very different from most. More will be added whenever I listen to and/or remember to add more.
